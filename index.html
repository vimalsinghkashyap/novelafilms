About Us

Novela Films is a young & dynamic production house with over 2 decades of collective experience. We are engaged in the production of Fictional & Non-Fictional content. Our rich experience across Platforms & Formats helps us to come up with unique solutions for our clients. We specialise in Documentaries, Corporate Videos, Branded Content, TV Shows, Web Series, Television Commercials, and Feature Films.

Our Services

Choose Your Service

Corporate Videos

Corporate video production refers to audio-visual corporate communications material (such as DVD, High-definition video, streaming video or other media). This may include product, service or company promotional videos, training videos and information videos.

Production

It Includes Budgeting, Insurance & Permits, Location Scouting – Domestic and International, Casting, Creative Crew, Hair, Makeup, Set Design, Props etc. On site Catering and Craft Service, Equipment, Security, Studio rental & Travel Arrangements.

Animation

2D animation, 3D animation, Typography Animation, Stop motion animation etc. Animation is all around us, be it your favourite TV commercials, Music Videos , Movies or even Corporate videos. A business has over 20 types of animated video types to choose from, according to their budget and creative requirement.

Education Technology

It includes Research , Scripting, Shooting & Post-Production. Education technology has grown by Leaps and bounds in the past few years. Be it KhanAcademy, HarvardHBX or our very own UpGrad, education had become much more accessible, facilitated by the 4G telecom revolution to a large extent. EdTech is primarily video based and we are at the fore front of this change. Novela Films helps you create visually engaging videos with easily comprehensible content

.

Advertising

Advertising is an audio or visual form of marketing communication that employs an openly sponsored, non-personal message to promote or sell a product, service or idea.Sponsors of advertising are often businesses wishing to promote their products or services. Advertising is differentiated from public relations in that an advertiser pays for and has control over the message.

Documentary

A documentary film is a nonfictional motion picture intended to document some aspect of reality, primarily for the purposes of instruction, education, or maintaining a historical record. Such films were originally shot on film stock—the only medium available—but now include video and digital productions that can be either direct-to-video, made into a TV show, or released for screening in cinemas.

Branded Content

End to End production of TV Programmes for Fiction, Non-Fiction and Branded Content. It Includes ideation, concept creation, idea development, story, screenplay, dialogue writing, pilot shoot, production planning, budgeting, casting, shooting of actual episodes & post-production.
